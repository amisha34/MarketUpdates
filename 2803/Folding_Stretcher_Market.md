 <p><strong>3. Accessibility and Maneuverability: Folding stretchers are particularly useful for situations where traditional stretchers may not be accessible or easily maneuverable.  <p><strong>4. Versatility and Multifunctionality: Folding stretchers can be used in various environments, including hospitals, clinics, sports events, outdoor activities, and industrial settings.  <p><strong>5. Durability and Strength: Folding stretchers are designed to withstand heavy loads and provide reliable support for patients.  <p><strong>6. Patient Comfort and Safety: Folding stretchers are designed to provide a comfortable and secure platform for patients during transport. They often come with features such as padded surfaces, adjustable straps, and safety restraints to ensure patient comfort and security during movement.</strong></p>
 
 
 
 <p><strong>7. Ease of Use: Folding stretchers are designed for easy and quick deployment, enabling medical personnel to rapidly assist and transfer patients. Their intuitive unfolding mechanism allows for swift setup and preparation, reducing response times in time-sensitive situations.</strong></p>
 
 
 
 <p><strong>8. Cost-effectiveness: Folding stretchers generally offer a cost-effective alternative compared to more complex and expensive patient transport systems. They provide a practical solution for efficient patient transport without compromising quality or functionality.</strong></p>
