 <p>Medicinal Gelatin Soft Capsules are a type of soft gelatin capsule that contain one or more active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs) and are used for oral administration. The gelatin shell of the capsule is typically made from animal-derived materials and is filled with a liquid or semi-solid formulation containing the API(s) and other excipients. These capsules are commonly used to deliver a wide range of medications, including vitamins, minerals, herbal supplements, and prescription drugs. The soft gelatin shell allows for easy swallowing and may also provide improved bioavailability of the drug compared to other dosage forms.</p>
