<h1>Global Rehabilitation Medical Device Market Research Report 2024-2030(Status and Outlook)</h1><br/><p>Rehabilitation medical devices are specialized equipment and tools designed to assist individuals in recovering lost physical functions due to injury, illness, or medical conditions. These devices help improve mobility, strength, coordination, and overall quality of life. Rehabilitation can address various functions such as movement, speech, cognition, swallowing, and continence, among others. Common rehabilitation devices include physiotherapy equipment, training equipment, and assessment devices, all of which are essential in medical and therapeutic settings.</p>
