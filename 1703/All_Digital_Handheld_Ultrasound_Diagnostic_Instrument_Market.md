 <p>The fully digital handheld ultrasound diagnostic instrument is a medical device that uses ultrasound technology to generate real-time images of internal organs and tissues. It is compact in design, lightweight, and easy to use, with all necessary components integrated into a handheld unit. The working principle of this device is to emit high-frequency sound waves into the body, which bounce back from the internal structure and tissues and are then detected by ultrasonic probes. The detector sends these signals to the computer, which processes the information and generates real-time images of the inspected area.</p>
