There are various types of modern dressings. Representative products include hydrogel dressings, hydrocolloid dressings, transparent thin dressings, foam dressings, alginate dressings, hydrophilic fiber dressings, mineral dressings, silver ion dressings and biological factor dressings. Traditional dressings have low cost and are widely used in clinical and daily use, but they do not have the function of moisturizing, and there is a risk of secondary infection when soaked. In the recovery process, the new granulation tissue is easy to grow into the gauze, and it is easy to cause damage when changing.
