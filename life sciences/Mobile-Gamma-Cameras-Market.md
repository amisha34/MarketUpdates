<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Mobile gamma cameras are portable medical imaging devices used for diagnostic purposes, particularly in nuclear medicine. These cameras enable clinicians to visualize and assess various internal bodily functions and structures by capturing images of gamma radiation emitted by radioactive substances administered to patients for diagnostic procedures.</p>
