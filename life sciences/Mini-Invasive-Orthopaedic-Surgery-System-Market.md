<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Mini Invasive Orthopaedic Surgery System refers to a surgical approach that utilizes small incisions, specialized instruments, and advanced imaging techniques to perform orthopaedic procedures with minimal damage to surrounding tissues. This innovative system aims to reduce patient recovery time, post-operative pain, and overall surgical complications.</p>
