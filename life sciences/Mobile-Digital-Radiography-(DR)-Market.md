<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Mobile Digital Radiography (DR) is an imaging technology that utilizes digital detectors to generate diagnostic images, allowing for quick and efficient medical imaging procedures. This technology offers enhanced image quality, immediate image availability, and the ability to manipulate images for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.</p>
