<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Minimally Invasive Surgical (MIS) Devices refer to advanced surgical instruments and equipment designed to perform minimally invasive procedures with reduced trauma to the body compared to traditional surgical techniques. These devices are crucial in modern healthcare settings for their precision and effectiveness.</p>

<h2>Market Dynamics:</h2>
<ul>

<li>Technological advancements in MIS devices, such as robotic-assisted surgeries, driving market growth.</li>
<li>Rising prevalence of chronic diseases leading to a higher adoption of minimally invasive surgical techniques.</li>
</ul>
