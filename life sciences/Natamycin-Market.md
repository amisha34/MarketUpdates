What is Natamycin?

Natamycin, also known by the brand name Natacyn, is a naturally occurring antifungal substance derived from Streptomyces natalensis. It appears as a white powder and works by inhibiting the growth of fungi, making it a popular choice for preventing fungal contamination in food and pharmaceutical products. Unlike many other antifungal agents, Natamycin is non-toxic to humans and has minimal impact on bacteria, making it a favorable option for a variety of applications.
