<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Single lumen microcatheters are minimally invasive medical devices used in various interventions to access and treat specific areas within the body. These catheters feature a single lumen, enabling precise delivery of medications, fluids, or contrast agents to targeted sites, enhancing procedural effectiveness and patient outcomes.</p>
