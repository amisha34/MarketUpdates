<p>Musculoskeletal Disorders involve issues with the bones, muscles, joints, ligaments, tendons, and nerves. These conditions can cause pain, stiffness, swelling, and limited movement. The treatment of musculoskeletal disorders aims to alleviate symptoms and improve the function of the affected area.</p>
