<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Gynecology Microwave Ablation involves using microwave energy to thermally ablate abnormal tissues in the female reproductive system, such as fibroids or polyps. This minimally invasive procedure offers an alternative to surgery for certain gynecological conditions, providing a less invasive and quicker recovery option for patients.</p>
