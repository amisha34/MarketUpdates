<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Natural Menthol and Synthetic Menthol are organic compounds derived either from peppermint or produced synthetically. These compounds are widely used in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, food and beverages, and more. Natural Menthol is extracted from peppermint oil, while Synthetic Menthol is manufactured through chemical synthesis.</p>
