<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Medical Stability Test Chambers are specialized equipment used for conducting stability tests on pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and other medical products under controlled environmental conditions. These chambers help in assessing the shelf life and storage stability of these products. They maintain specific temperature, humidity, and lighting conditions to simulate real-world storage environments accurately.</p>
