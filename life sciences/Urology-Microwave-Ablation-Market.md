<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Urology Microwave Ablation is a minimally invasive procedure that uses microwave energy to destroy abnormal tissues within the urinary system, particularly in the treatment of prostate, kidney, and bladder cancers. This technique offers precise targeting of tumors while minimizing damage to surrounding healthy tissues, providing a promising alternative to traditional surgical interventions.</p>
