<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Multi-chamber bags for parenteral nutrition are specialized medical bags with multiple compartments designed to deliver different components of parenteral nutrition simultaneously to patients. These bags are crucial for patients who require intravenous feeding due to an inability to consume nutrients orally. The technology in these bags allows for customized nutrient delivery based on individual patient needs and medical conditions.</p>
