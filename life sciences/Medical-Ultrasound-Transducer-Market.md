<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Medical Ultrasound Transducers are widely used in diagnostic imaging procedures to visualize internal organs and tissues. They emit high-frequency sound waves that reflect off body structures to create images used by medical professionals for diagnostic purposes. These devices play a crucial role in the field of healthcare by enabling non-invasive imaging.</p>
