<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Lymphangioleiomyomatosis (LAM) is a rare, progressive lung disease that primarily affects women of childbearing age. It is characterized by the growth of abnormal muscle cells in the lungs, which can lead to breathing difficulties and lung function impairment.</p>
