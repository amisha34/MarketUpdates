<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Lyophilized injectable drugs refer to medications that have been freeze-dried to increase their shelf stability and facilitate easier transportation and administration. This process involves removing the solvent from the drug under low temperature and pressure, resulting in a dry and highly stable product that can be reconstituted with a suitable diluent before administration.</p>
