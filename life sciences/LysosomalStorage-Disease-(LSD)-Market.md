<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Lysosomal Storage Diseases are a group of inherited metabolic disorders caused by deficiencies in lysosomal enzymes, leading to the accumulation of undigested substrates within cells. These diseases often result in progressive health issues affecting various organs and systems. Understanding these diseases is crucial for effective management and treatment.</p>
