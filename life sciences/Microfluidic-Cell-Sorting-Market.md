<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Microfluidic cell sorting involves the separation and isolation of specific cells based on their properties within microfluidic channels, enabling precise and efficient cell analysis and sorting processes. This technology has revolutionized various sectors, including research, diagnostics, and therapeutics, by offering high throughput and accuracy in cell sorting methodologies.</p>
