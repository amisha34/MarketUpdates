<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Mobile Imaging refers to the use of smartphones and other mobile devices to capture, edit, and share images and videos. It encompasses a range of technologies and applications that enhance the capabilities of mobile cameras and improve the quality of images captured on these devices. With advancements in mobile imaging technology, users can now achieve professional-grade photography and videography using their smartphones.</p>
