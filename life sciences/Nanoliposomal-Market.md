<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Nanoliposomal lutein refers to a form of lutein that has been encapsulated in liposomes, which are tiny spherical vesicles with a lipid bilayer. This encapsulation enhances the bioavailability and stability of lutein, a carotenoid with known benefits for eye health and antioxidant properties.</p>
