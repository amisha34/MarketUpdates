<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Annuloplasty Repair Devices are medical devices used in the surgical repair of heart valves, specifically in cases of mitral or tricuspid valve regurgitation. These devices help to remodel and support the mitral annulus, improving valve function and restoring proper cardiac output.</p>
