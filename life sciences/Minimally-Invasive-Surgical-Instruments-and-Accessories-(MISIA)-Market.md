<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Minimally Invasive Surgical Instruments and Accessories (MISIA) refer to tools and equipment used during minimally invasive surgeries, which involve less trauma to the body compared to traditional open surgeries. These instruments aid in performing complex procedures through small incisions, leading to reduced scarring, faster recovery times, and fewer complications.</p>
