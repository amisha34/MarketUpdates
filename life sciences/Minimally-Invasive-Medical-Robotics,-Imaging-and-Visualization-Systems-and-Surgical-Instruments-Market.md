<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Minimally Invasive Medical Robotics, Imaging and Visualization Systems and Surgical Instruments refer to advanced technologies and instruments used in minimally invasive procedures to enhance precision, reduce recovery time, and improve patient outcomes. These systems involve robotics, imaging modalities, and visualization tools that enable surgeons to perform complex surgeries through small incisions.</p>
