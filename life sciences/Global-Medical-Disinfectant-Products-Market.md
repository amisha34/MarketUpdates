<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Medical disinfectant products refer to chemicals or substances used to kill pathogenic microorganisms on surfaces in healthcare settings to prevent infection spread. These products play a vital role in maintaining hygiene standards in hospitals, clinics, and other medical facilities to ensure patient and staff safety.</p>
