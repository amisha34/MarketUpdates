<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Mineral Trioxide Aggregate (MTA) is a bioactive endodontic cement composed of fine hydrophilic particles that set in the presence of moisture, used primarily in dental treatments for various procedures like root canal treatments, pulp capping, and perforation repair.</p>
