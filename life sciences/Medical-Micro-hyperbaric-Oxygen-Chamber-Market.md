<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>The Medical Micro-hyperbaric Oxygen Chamber is a therapeutic device built to deliver concentrated oxygen to a patient in a pressurized environment. It aids in various medical treatments by enhancing oxygen supply to damaged tissues for accelerated healing and recovery, offering a non-invasive and safe solution for medical professionals. </p>
