<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Neuro-endoscopy is a minimally invasive surgical procedure that involves the use of an endoscope to diagnose and treat neurological conditions. It allows neurosurgeons to access deep-seated brain lesions through small incisions, reducing the need for traditional open surgeries and leading to faster recovery times for patients.</p>
