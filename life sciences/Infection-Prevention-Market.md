<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Infection prevention refers to the measures and practices aimed at preventing the spread of infectious diseases within healthcare settings and the broader community. This includes the use of protocols, procedures, and products to minimize the risk of infections and enhance patient and staff safety.</p>
