<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Military navigation instruments comprise GPS systems, inertial navigation systems, and radar equipment designed for use by armed forces to enhance situational awareness, target acquisition, and overall battlefield effectiveness. These devices provide critical location data, detailed mapping, and navigation assistance to military personnel in various operational scenarios.</p>
