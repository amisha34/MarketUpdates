<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Lys-C Protease, also known as lysyl endopeptidase, is a proteolytic enzyme widely used in research and biotechnological applications. It cleaves peptide bonds at the carboxyl side of lysine residues, offering precise proteolytic cleavage.</p>
