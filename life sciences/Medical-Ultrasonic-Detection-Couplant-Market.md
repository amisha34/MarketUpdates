<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Medical Ultrasonic Detection Couplant refers to the gel or other medium used to enable the efficient transmission of ultrasonic waves through the skin and into the body for medical imaging purposes. This couplant ensures optimal contact between the ultrasound probe and the patient's skin, facilitating accurate and clear imaging results.</p>
