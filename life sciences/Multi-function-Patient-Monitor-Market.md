<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>A multi function patient monitor is a medical device that measures a patient's vital signs, such as heart rate, blood pressure, and oxygen saturation. It provides continuous monitoring of a patient's health status and enables healthcare providers to track changes in real-time to ensure timely interventions.</p>
