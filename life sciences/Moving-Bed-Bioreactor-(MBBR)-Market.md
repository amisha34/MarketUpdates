<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>The Moving Bed Bioreactor (MBBR) is a wastewater treatment process that utilizes a floating media bed to enhance the performance of the treatment system. It provides a highly efficient method for removing organic and inorganic contaminants from water, making it a widely adopted technology in the environmental engineering sector.</p>
