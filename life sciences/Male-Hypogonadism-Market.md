<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Male Hypogonadism is a condition characterized by the body's inability to produce sufficient amounts of testosterone, impacting physical and mental health. It can lead to various symptoms such as fatigue, erectile dysfunction, and reduced muscle mass. Effective diagnosis and treatment are essential to manage this condition.</p>
