<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>A Mobile Electrocardiograph Monitor is a portable, easy-to-use device that enables individuals to monitor their heart's electrical activity in real-time. It provides valuable insights into heart health and can help in the early detection of cardiac abnormalities.</p>
