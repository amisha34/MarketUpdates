<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Neural networks, a subset of artificial intelligence (AI), are a system of algorithms modeled after the human brain's structure and function. They process large amounts of complex data to identify patterns and relationships, enabling machines to learn and make decisions autonomously. This technology has widespread applications across various industries, driving innovation and efficiency in tasks like image and speech recognition, financial forecasting, and healthcare diagnostics.</p>
