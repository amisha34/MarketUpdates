<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Contrast agents for magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) are substances used to enhance the visibility of internal body structures during an MRI scan. These agents help in improving the quality of images obtained from MRI scans, aiding in accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.</p>
