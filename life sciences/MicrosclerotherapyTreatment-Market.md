<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Microsclerotherapy Treatment is a minimally invasive procedure used to effectively treat small, superficial varicose veins and spider veins. The treatment involves injecting a sclerosing agent into the affected veins, causing them to close and eventually fade away. This procedure is commonly preferred due to its high success rates and minimal downtime.</p>
