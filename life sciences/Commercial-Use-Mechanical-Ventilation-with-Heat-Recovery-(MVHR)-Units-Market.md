<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Commercial Use Mechanical Ventilation with Heat Recovery (MVHR) Units refer to advanced systems designed for efficient air exchange in commercial buildings while conserving energy by recovering and reusing heat from the outgoing air. These units help maintain optimal indoor air quality and reduce heating costs, making them essential components of modern HVAC systems.</p>
