<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>A medical flat panel detector is an imaging tool used in various medical imaging devices such as X-ray systems and cone-beam computed tomography (CBCT) systems. It leverages thin-film transistor (TFT) technology along with digital image sensors to capture high-quality diagnostic images efficiently.</p>
