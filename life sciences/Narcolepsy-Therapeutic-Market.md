<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Narcolepsy Therapeutics refer to the various treatments and medications aimed at managing the symptoms of narcolepsy, a chronic neurological disorder that affects the brain's ability to regulate sleep-wake cycles. These therapeutics help improve wakefulness and reduce excessive daytime sleepiness in individuals with narcolepsy.</p>
