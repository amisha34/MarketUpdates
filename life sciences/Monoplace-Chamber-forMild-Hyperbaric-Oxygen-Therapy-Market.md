<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Monoplace chambers are designed for the administration of mild hyperbaric oxygen therapy, a non-invasive procedure that involves breathing pure oxygen in a pressurized chamber at a level higher than sea level atmospheric pressure. This therapy is known for its potential benefits in treating various medical conditions.</p>
