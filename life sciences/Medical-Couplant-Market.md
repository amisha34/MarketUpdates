<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Medical couplants are specialized gel-like substances used to optimize the transmission of ultrasonic waves between the probe and the object being examined during medical procedures such as ultrasounds and echocardiograms. These couplants help minimize air gaps and ensure accurate imaging results by facilitating the passage of acoustic energy.</p>
