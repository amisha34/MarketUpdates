N,N-Diethyl-meta-toluamide, commonly referred to as DEET or diethyltoluamide, is a chemical compound widely used as the active ingredient in insect repellents. It provides effective protection against mosquito bites and other insect vectors, making it a critical component in products designed for personal use in both urban and rural environments. The DEET Repellents market encompasses a variety of products, including creams, lotions, and aerosols, tailored to meet diverse consumer needs.
