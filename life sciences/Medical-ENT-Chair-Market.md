<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Medical ENT Chairs are specialized chairs designed for otolaryngology (ENT) examinations and procedures. These chairs provide comfort and support for patients during ear, nose, and throat examinations and treatments, allowing medical professionals easy access to the affected areas while ensuring patient safety and comfort.</p>
