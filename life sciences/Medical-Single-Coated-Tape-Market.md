<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Medical single coated tape refers to a type of adhesive tape that has adhesive applied to only one side of the backing material. This tape is commonly used in medical applications such as wound care, surgical procedures, and medical device assembly, providing secure and gentle adhesion.</p>
