<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Neurointervention flow-diverting stents are specialized medical devices designed to treat intracranial aneurysms and other vascular conditions by diverting blood flow, ultimately promoting vessel healing and preventing potential ruptures. These stents are essential tools in neurointerventional procedures, contributing to improved patient outcomes and long-term efficacy.</p>
