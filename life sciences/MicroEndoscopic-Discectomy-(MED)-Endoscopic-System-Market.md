    <h2>Definition:</h2>
    <p>MicroEndoscopic Discectomy (MED) Endoscopic System is a minimally invasive surgical procedure used to treat herniated discs in the spine. This procedure involves the use of an endoscope to visualize and remove the damaged disc material with small incisions, resulting in quicker recovery times and reduced post-operative complications.</p>
