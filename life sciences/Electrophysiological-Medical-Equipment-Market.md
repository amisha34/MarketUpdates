<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Electrophysiological Medical Equipment refers to devices utilized in measuring and analyzing the electrical activity of various organs in the body, primarily the heart and brain, aiding in the diagnosis of abnormalities and disorders. These instruments are pivotal in providing valuable insights into the functioning of vital systems.</p>
