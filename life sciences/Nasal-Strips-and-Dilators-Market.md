Nasal Strips and Dilators: The Basics
Nasal strips and dilators are products designed to improve airflow through the nasal passages, addressing a variety of issues such as nasal congestion, snoring, and sleep apnea. Nasal strips are adhesive devices placed on the nose to physically open the nasal passages, providing relief for conditions like blocked nostrils or difficulty breathing through the nose. Nasal dilators, on the other hand, are small devices typically worn inside the nostrils to prevent the nasal passages from collapsing, promoting easier breathing.

As part of the healthcare and medical device industry, the nasal strips and dilators market plays a vital role in improving patient quality of life. These products have been particularly useful for individuals suffering from respiratory disorders, allergies, and sleep-related issues.

Manufacturers are continually improving the design and functionality of nasal strips and dilators.
