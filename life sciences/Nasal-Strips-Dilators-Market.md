<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Nasal strips and dilators are devices used to manage nasal congestion and improve airflow through the nasal passages. They are commonly utilized by individuals suffering from conditions like snoring, sleep apnea, allergies, and nasal congestion to enhance breathing and alleviate symptoms.</p>
