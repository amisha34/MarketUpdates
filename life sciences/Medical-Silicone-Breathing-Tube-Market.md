<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Medical Silicone Breathing Tubes are essential medical devices used in respiratory therapy to provide oxygen or assist with breathing. These tubes are typically made of high-quality silicone material, ensuring flexibility and biocompatibility for patient comfort and safety.</p>
