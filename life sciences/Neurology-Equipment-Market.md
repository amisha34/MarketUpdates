<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Neurology equipment refers to specialized medical devices used in diagnosing and treating neurological disorders affecting the brain, spinal cord, and nerves. These equipment are essential for neurologists in conducting various tests, monitoring brain activity, and performing surgical procedures related to neurological conditions. With technological advancements, neurology equipment has become more sophisticated, offering precise diagnosis and treatment options.</p>
