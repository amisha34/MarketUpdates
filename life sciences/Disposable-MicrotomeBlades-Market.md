<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Disposable Microtome Blades are cutting tools used in histology and pathology labs for slicing tissue samples thinly and precisely for examination under a microscope. The blades are designed for single-use to ensure optimal performance and reduce the risk of contamination.</p>
