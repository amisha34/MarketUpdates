The Split Septum Needleless Connector market includes specialized medical devices designed to prevent needlestick injuries by eliminating the use of needles when accessing intravascular catheters. These connectors are primarily used in healthcare settings to facilitate safe and efficient intravenous access, ensuring the protection of healthcare workers from accidental needle injuries. The two main types of needleless connectors in use are the standard split septum connectors and the luer-activated mechanical valve connectors. These devices play a crucial role in improving patient safety, reducing healthcare-associated infections, and enhancing the efficiency of medical procedures.
