<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Myasthenia Gravis is a neurological autoimmune disorder that causes muscle weakness and fatigue, impacting the voluntary muscles in the body. It occurs when the communication between nerves and muscles is disrupted, leading to muscle weakness and fatigue. This condition can affect various muscle groups, including those responsible for breathing and swallowing.</p>
