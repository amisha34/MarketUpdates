<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Morgue refrigerators are specially designed refrigeration units used in morgues and mortuaries to store deceased bodies at low temperatures, preserving them for extended periods.</p>
<p>These refrigerators are essential for maintaining the integrity of bodies during autopsies, investigations, and further processing.</p>
