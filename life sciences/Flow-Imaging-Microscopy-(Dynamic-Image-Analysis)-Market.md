<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Flow Imaging Microscopy (Dynamic Image Analysis) refers to a technique that combines microscopic imaging capabilities with fluid dynamics to analyze and study particles in various liquid samples. It enables real-time visualization and measurement of particle size, shape, and count within a fluid medium.</p>
