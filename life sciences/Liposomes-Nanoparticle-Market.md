<p>Liposomes nanoparticles are lipid-based nanoparticles used in drug delivery systems to enhance therapeutic efficacy and reduce side effects. By encapsulating drugs within lipid bilayers, liposomes enable targeted delivery and controlled release of pharmaceutical compounds, revolutionizing treatment approaches in various medical fields.</p>
