<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Medical Grade N95 and KN95 respirator masks are personal protective equipment designed to provide a high level of respiratory protection against airborne particles, viruses, and bacteria. Both N95 and KN95 masks meet specific filtration standards set by regulatory bodies to ensure their effectiveness in filtering out harmful contaminants. These masks are widely used in healthcare settings and industrial environments to safeguard individuals from respiratory hazards.</p>
