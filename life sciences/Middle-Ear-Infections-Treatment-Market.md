<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Middle ear infections, also known as otitis media, refer to the inflammation of the middle ear, commonly caused by viral or bacterial infections. This condition can lead to pain, discharge from the ear, and potential hearing loss if left untreated. Middle ear infections can affect individuals of all ages, with children being more prone to experiencing them. Effective treatment strategies play a crucial role in managing middle ear infections and preventing complications.</p>
