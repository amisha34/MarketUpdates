<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>At-home genetic testing refers to the practice of testing individuals for genetic conditions or predispositions in the comfort of their own homes, using self-collection kits that are then sent to laboratories for analysis.</p>
<p>This approach provides individuals with valuable information about their genetic makeup without the need for visits to healthcare facilities.</p>
