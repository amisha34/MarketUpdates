<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Molecular biology reagents and kits are essential tools used in biological research to study the structures, functions, and interactions of biomolecules such as DNA, RNA, and proteins. These reagents facilitate various molecular biology experiments, including DNA amplification, purification, sequencing, and cloning.</p>
