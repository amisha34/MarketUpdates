<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Nano based drug delivery system refers to the administration of therapeutic agents at the nanoscale level, enhancing their stability, solubility, and targeted delivery to specific sites within the body. This cutting-edge technology offers precise control over drug release mechanisms, improving efficacy and minimizing side effects. Nano based drug delivery systems encompass a wide range of nanoparticles designed to encapsulate, protect, and transport drugs to their intended destinations.</p>
