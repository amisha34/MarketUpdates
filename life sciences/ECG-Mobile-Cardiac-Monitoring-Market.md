<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>ECG Mobile Cardiac Monitoring refers to the continuous monitoring of a patient's heart activity using a portable device that records electrocardiogram (ECG) signals. These devices provide real-time data on the heart's electrical activity, enabling healthcare professionals to diagnose various heart conditions remotely.</p>
