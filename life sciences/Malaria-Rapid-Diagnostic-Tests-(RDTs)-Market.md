    <h2>Definition:</h2>
    <p>The Malaria Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDTs) are diagnostic tools used to quickly and accurately detect the presence of malaria parasites in a patient's blood sample. These tests play a crucial role in prompt diagnosis and treatment of malaria infections, aiding in reducing transmission rates and improving patient outcomes.</p>
