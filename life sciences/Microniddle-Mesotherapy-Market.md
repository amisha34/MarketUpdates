<p>Microniddle Mesotherapy refers to a minimally invasive cosmetic procedure that involves the use of microneedles to introduce vitamins, minerals, and other therapeutic agents into the skin's dermis. This technique is primarily used for rejuvenation, hydration, and revitalization of the skin, aiding in improving its overall appearance and health.</p>
