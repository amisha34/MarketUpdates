<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Vaccine Particulate Adjuvants are agents added to vaccines to enhance the body's immune response to the vaccine, often by improving antigen presentation or promoting inflammation. These adjuvants play a crucial role in modern vaccine development by boosting the effectiveness of vaccines against infectious diseases.</p>
