<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Membrane Technology in the Pharmaceutical industry involves the use of selective semi-permeable membranes to separate molecules and particles for various pharmaceutical applications. This technology plays a crucial role in drug manufacturing processes such as filtration, purification, concentration, and separation.</p>
