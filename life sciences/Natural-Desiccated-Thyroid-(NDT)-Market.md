**Definition:**

<p>Natural Desiccated Thyroid (NDT) refers to a medication made from the desiccated (dried and powdered) thyroid gland of animals, typically pigs. It contains the thyroid hormones T3 (triiodothyronine) and T4 (thyroxine) in the same ratio as naturally produced by the human thyroid gland. NDT is commonly used in the treatment of hypothyroidism, a condition characterized by an underactive thyroid gland.</p>
