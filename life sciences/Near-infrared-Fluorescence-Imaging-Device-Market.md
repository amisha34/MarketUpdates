<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Near-infrared Fluorescence (NIRF) Imaging Device is a cutting-edge technology that utilizes near-infrared light to visualize molecular information in biological systems. It enables real-time imaging of biological processes at the molecular level, offering non-invasive, high-resolution imaging capabilities that are crucial in various medical and research applications.</p>
