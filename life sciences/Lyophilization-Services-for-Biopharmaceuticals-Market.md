<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Lyophilization, also known as freeze-drying, is a process used in the pharmaceutical industry for the preservation of sensitive biological molecules by removing water content under controlled conditions. This technique ensures the stability and extended shelf-life of biopharmaceutical products.</p>
