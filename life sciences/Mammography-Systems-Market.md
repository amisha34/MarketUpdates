<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Mammography Systems are specialized imaging systems used for breast examinations to detect and diagnose breast cancer and other abnormalities. These systems typically utilize low-dose X-rays for high-resolution images of the breast tissue. The Mammography Systems market encompasses various equipment, software, and services related to breast imaging technologies.</p>
