<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Mercury spill kits are specialized containment and cleanup kits designed for controlling and eliminating mercury spills, minimizing environmental contamination and health risks associated with mercury exposure. These kits typically include absorbent materials, protective gear, disposal containers, and instructions for safe handling.</p>
