<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Microwave Diathermy Units are therapeutic devices that utilize electromagnetic waves to generate heat within body tissues, aiding in the treatment of various musculoskeletal conditions by promoting vasodilation and improving blood flow. These units are extensively used in physical therapy and rehabilitation settings.</p>
