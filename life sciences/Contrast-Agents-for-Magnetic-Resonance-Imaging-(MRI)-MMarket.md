<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Contrast agents for Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) are substances administered to patients undergoing MRI scans to enhance the visibility of internal structures and improve the diagnostic accuracy of the images produced. These agents alter the magnetic properties of tissues, allowing for clearer imaging and better differentiation of abnormalities.</p>
