<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Starting with a concise definition, molecular weight markers are essential tools used in molecular biology and biochemistry to determine the approximate size of DNA, RNA, and protein samples during electrophoresis. These markers consist of a mixture of known DNA fragments or proteins with specific molecular weights and are crucial for accurate size determination in various laboratory analyses.</p>
