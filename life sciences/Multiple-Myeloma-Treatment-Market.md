<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Multiple Myeloma is a type of cancer that forms in a type of white blood cell called a plasma cell. These cancerous cells accumulate in the bone marrow, crowding out healthy blood cells. The condition weakens the immune system and can lead to anemia, bone pain, kidney damage, and other serious health problems.</p>
