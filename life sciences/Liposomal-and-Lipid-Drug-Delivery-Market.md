<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>In the pharmaceutical field, Liposomal and Lipid Drug Delivery Systems involve the use of lipid-based nanocarriers to enhance drug delivery efficiency and therapeutic outcomes. These systems encapsulate drugs in lipid bilayers or vesicles, allowing for targeted and controlled drug release.</p>
