<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Nebulizer inhalers are medical devices that convert liquid medication into a mist for inhalation, aiding in the treatment of respiratory conditions such as asthma, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), and cystic fibrosis. These devices are commonly used to deliver bronchodilators, corticosteroids, and other respiratory medications directly into the lungs.</p>
