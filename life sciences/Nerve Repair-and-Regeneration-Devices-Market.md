<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Nerve repair and regeneration devices are medical tools and technologies used to repair, regenerate, and restore damaged or injured nerves in the body. These devices play a crucial role in enhancing the functional recovery of patients with nerve injuries by promoting nerve growth and connectivity.</p>

<h2>Market Dynamics:</h2>

</ul>
