<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Medical Topical Hemostatic Agents are products used to control bleeding by promoting clotting either on the surface of a wound or inside the body. These agents are crucial in surgical procedures, trauma care, and other medical contexts where rapid and effective hemorrhage control is necessary. The market for these agents encompasses various product types designed for diverse applications, emphasizing their importance in healthcare settings.</p>
