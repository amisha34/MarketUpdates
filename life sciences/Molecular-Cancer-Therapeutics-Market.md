<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Molecular Cancer Therapeutics refers to the development and application of targeted therapies at the molecular level to treat various types of cancer by focusing on specific molecules involved in cancer growth and progression. These therapies aim to enhance treatment efficacy while minimizing adverse effects on healthy cells.</p>
