<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Infection Control Devices are instruments, equipment, and products aimed at preventing the spread of infections in various healthcare settings such as hospitals, clinics, and laboratories. These devices play a crucial role in maintaining a safe and hygienic environment to protect patients, healthcare workers, and visitors from potential infections.</p>
