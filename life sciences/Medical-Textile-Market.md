<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Medical textiles refer to textile materials used in healthcare and medical applications such as surgical gowns, drapes, bandages, implants, etc. These textiles are designed to provide specific functionalities to aid in medical procedures and patient care.</p>

<h2>Market Dynamics:</h2>
<ul>
<li>Increasing demand for non-invasive medical procedures driving the adoption of medical textiles in wound care and orthopedic applications.</li>
<li>Technological advancements leading to the development of smart textiles for monitoring and diagnostics in healthcare settings.</li>
<li>Rising awareness regarding infection control measures boosting the utilization of antimicrobial textiles in hospitals and clinics.</li>
</ul>
