<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Multifunctional Water Analysis Instrumentation refers to advanced instruments used for assessing various parameters in water samples, such as pH levels, dissolved oxygen, turbidity, and conductivity. These instruments play a crucial role in ensuring water quality standards are met in various industries, including wastewater treatment plants, laboratories, and environmental monitoring agencies.</p>
