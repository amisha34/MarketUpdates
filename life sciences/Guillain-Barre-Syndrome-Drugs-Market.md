<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Guillain-Barre Syndrome (GBS) is a rare neurological disorder where the body's immune system mistakenly attacks its nerves, leading to muscle weakness and paralysis. It can be life-threatening but is treatable with prompt medical intervention involving various drugs and therapies.</p>
