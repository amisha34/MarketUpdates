<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>A Mobile ECG Telemetry Device is a portable electrocardiogram instrument that wirelessly transmits real-time heart activity data to healthcare providers or monitoring systems, enabling remote patient monitoring and timely intervention.</p>

</ul>
