**<h2>Definition:</h2>**

Melatonin is a hormone produced naturally by the pineal gland in response to darkness. It plays a crucial role in regulating the sleep-wake cycle, also known as the circadian rhythm. Melatonin supplements are often used to improve sleep disorders, alleviate jet lag, and manage insomnia. The market for melatonin products has been witnessing steady growth due to the rising demand for sleep enhancement solutions worldwide.
