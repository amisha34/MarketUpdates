<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>In the global healthcare market, Multi-Function Blood Glucose Meter Systems refer to advanced devices that provide a combination of functionalities beyond traditional blood glucose monitoring. These systems offer features such as insulin dose calculation, connectivity to mobile apps for data tracking, and personalized insights for diabetic patients to manage their condition effectively.</p>
