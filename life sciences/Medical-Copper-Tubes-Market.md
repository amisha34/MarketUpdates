<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Medical copper tubes refer to tubes made from copper that are specifically designed and manufactured for use in medical applications. These tubes are known for their excellent thermal conductivity and corrosion resistance, making them ideal for medical equipment and devices.</p>
