<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Medical grade silicone tubes are specialized tubes made from high-quality silicone material that are used in various medical applications such as delivery of fluids, gases, and other substances within the human body or medical equipment. These tubes are designed to offer biocompatibility, flexibility, and resistance to extreme temperatures.</p>
