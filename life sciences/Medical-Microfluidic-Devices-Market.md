<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Medical microfluidic devices are precision-engineered systems that manipulate fluids at the microscale to facilitate various medical applications, including diagnostics, drug delivery, and point-of-care testing. These devices integrate microchannels, sensors, and actuators to perform complex functions with high accuracy and efficiency.</p>
