<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Healthcare and Medical Simulators are advanced technological tools used in medical training and patient simulations to replicate real-life scenarios and enhance learning outcomes for healthcare professionals. These simulators can range from virtual reality systems to task trainers and can simulate various medical procedures and situations. By providing a realistic practice environment, medical simulators play a crucial role in improving clinical skills and confidence among medical professionals.</p>
