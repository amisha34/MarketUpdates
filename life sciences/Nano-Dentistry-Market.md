Nano-Dentistry refers to the application of nanotechnology in dental care and treatment, utilizing nano-materials and nano-robots to achieve nearly perfect oral hygiene. This innovative approach introduces advanced solutions such as dentition renaturalization, local anesthesia, curing hypersensitivity, orthodontic realignment, and maintenance of oral health through nanorobotic dentifrice that eliminates caries-causing bacteria. Nano-Dentistry has revolutionized the dental industry by enabling precise, efficient, and minimally invasive treatments, enhancing the overall patient experience.
