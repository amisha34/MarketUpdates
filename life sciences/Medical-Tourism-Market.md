<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Medical Tourism refers to the practice of traveling to a foreign country to receive medical treatment or care. This phenomenon has gained increasing popularity over recent years due to various factors such as cost-effectiveness, quality of care, and access to specialized procedures.</p>
