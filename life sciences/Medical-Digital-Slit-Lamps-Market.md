<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Medical Digital Slit Lamps are specialized ophthalmic instruments used by eye care professionals to examine the anterior segment of the eye thoroughly. These devices provide high-resolution images of the eye, aiding in the diagnosis and treatment of various eye conditions.</p>
