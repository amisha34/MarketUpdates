<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Mud Pressure Gauges are instruments used in drilling operations to measure the pressure applied by the mud circulating in the wellbore. These gauges are critical for monitoring and controlling the drilling process to ensure optimal efficiency and safety.</p>
