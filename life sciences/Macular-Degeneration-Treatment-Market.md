<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Macular Degeneration, also known as Age-related Macular Degeneration (AMD), is a progressive eye disease affecting the macula, the central part of the retina, leading to vision loss in the center of the field of vision. It is the leading cause of vision impairment and blindness among individuals aged 50 and older.</p>
