<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Medical exercise equipment refers to specialized devices and tools designed for the rehabilitation and enhancement of physical health and wellness. These equipment are utilized in medical facilities such as hospitals, clinics, and rehabilitation centers to aid patients in recovering from injuries, surgeries, or chronic conditions through targeted exercise programs.</p>
