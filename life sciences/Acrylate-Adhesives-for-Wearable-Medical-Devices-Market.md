<p>Acrylate adhesives in wearable medical devices refer to a type of adhesive material that offers strong bonding capabilities, flexibility, and biocompatibility for attaching medical devices to the skin securely. These adhesives are specifically designed to adhere well to various surfaces while being gentle on the skin, making them ideal for use in wearable medical devices.</p>
