<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Minimally Invasive Glaucoma Surgery (MIGS) Devices are innovative medical tools used in ophthalmology to treat glaucoma with minimal disruption to the eye's anatomy. These devices aim to reduce intraocular pressure and provide effective glaucoma management.</p>
