<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Micronutrients for Feed refer to essential vitamins and minerals required in trace amounts by animals for optimal growth, development, and overall health. These nutrients play a crucial role in ensuring the proper functioning of metabolic processes and immune responses in livestock and poultry.</p>
