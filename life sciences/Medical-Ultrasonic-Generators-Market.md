<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Medical Ultrasonic Generators are devices that utilize ultrasonic waves for various medical applications, including imaging, diagnostic procedures, surgery, and therapy. These generators produce high-frequency sound waves beyond the range of human hearing, enabling precise and non-invasive medical procedures.</p>
