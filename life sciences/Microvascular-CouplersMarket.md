<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Microvascular couplers are medical devices used in microsurgery to facilitate the joining of small blood vessels. These devices play a crucial role in connecting blood vessels during reconstructive surgeries, aiding in the restoration of blood flow to tissues.</p>
