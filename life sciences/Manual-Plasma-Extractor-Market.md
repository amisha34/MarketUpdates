<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>The manual plasma extractor is a medical device used to separate plasma from blood samples efficiently, allowing for various diagnostic and therapeutic procedures. This equipment plays a crucial role in laboratories, hospitals, and blood banks by enabling the isolation and collection of plasma for further analysis.</p>
