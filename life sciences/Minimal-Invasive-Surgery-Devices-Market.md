<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Minimal Invasive Surgery Devices refer to advanced medical devices used in minimally invasive procedures that involve small incisions, leading to reduced scarring, shorter recovery times, and lower risks for patients. These devices are designed to perform intricate surgeries in a less invasive manner.</p>
