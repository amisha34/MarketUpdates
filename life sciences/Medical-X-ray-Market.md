<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Medical X-ray is a common diagnostic imaging technique that uses electromagnetic radiation to create images of the internal structures of the human body. It is widely used in medical settings to diagnose various conditions and aid in treatment planning. The technology has evolved over the years, offering improved image quality and reduced radiation exposure risks.
