<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Medical Videoscopes refer to medical devices that utilize a small camera attached to a flexible tube to visualize internal body parts for diagnostic and surgical purposes. These devices play a crucial role in modern healthcare by enabling physicians to perform minimally invasive procedures with high precision.</p>
