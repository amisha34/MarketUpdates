<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Medical rubber gloves are essential protective equipment widely used in healthcare settings to ensure the safety of both patients and healthcare professionals. These gloves are primarily made of latex, nitrile, or vinyl materials, offering barriers against contaminants and infectious agents.</p>

<h2>Market Dynamics:</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Increasing emphasis on infection control measures drives the demand for medical rubber gloves globally. The recent COVID-19 pandemic has further highlighted the importance of these gloves in preventing the spread of diseases and viruses.</li>
  <li>Technological advancements in manufacturing processes have led to the development of more durable and comfortable gloves, enhancing user experience and compliance.</li>
  <li>Rising healthcare expenditure, coupled with growing awareness about the importance of hand hygiene, continues to propel the market for medical rubber gloves.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Market Trends:</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Shift towards eco-friendly and biodegradable glove materials to reduce environmental impact.</li>

  <li>Growing trend of online purchase and direct-to-customer distribution channels for medical gloves, providing convenience and accessibility to buyers.</li>
</ul>
