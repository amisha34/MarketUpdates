<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Healthcare Simulation involves simulating real healthcare scenarios for training and education purposes in a controlled environment. It enables healthcare professionals, students, and researchers to practice and enhance their clinical skills without risk to patients. Through simulated scenarios ranging from basic to complex, participants can improve their decision-making, communication, and technical skills in a safe and effective manner.</p>
