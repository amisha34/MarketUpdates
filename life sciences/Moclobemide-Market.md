<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Moclobemide is a reversible inhibitor of monoamine oxidase type A (RIMA) antidepressant. It is utilized in the treatment of depression and social anxiety disorders among adults. Moclobemide offers a safer profile compared to irreversible monoamine oxidase inhibitors (MAOIs) due to its reversible nature, making it a preferred choice in clinical practice.</p>
