<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Medical Oxygen Concentrators and Cylinders are devices used to provide supplemental oxygen to patients with respiratory conditions, by concentrating oxygen from the surrounding air. These devices are essential in delivering oxygen therapy to individuals in need, helping them breathe more easily and improve their overall health.</p>
