<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Medical suction trolleys are essential equipment used in healthcare facilities to assist in the aspiration and removal of bodily fluids, mucus, and other substances from a patient's airway or surgical site. These trolleys are designed for efficient mobility and ease of use during medical procedures.</p>
