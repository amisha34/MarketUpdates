<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Medical sensor patches are innovative devices that monitor various health metrics non-invasively through sensor technology embedded in patch formats applied to the skin. These patches are designed to provide continuous monitoring of vital signs and biometric data for medical purposes efficiently.</p>
