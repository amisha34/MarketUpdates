<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>The Meningococcal Group B Vaccine is a crucial immunization that helps protect against meningococcal disease caused by the bacterium Neisseria meningitidis serogroup B.</p>
<p>This vaccine is designed to stimulate the body's immune response to fight off potential infections and is an integral part of public health initiatives worldwide.</p>
