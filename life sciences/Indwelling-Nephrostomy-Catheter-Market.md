<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Indwelling nephrostomy catheters are medical devices used in the drainage of urine from the kidneys to an external collection system. These catheters are implanted into the renal pelvis through the skin and provide a means of urinary diversion in cases of obstruction or other kidney-related issues.</p>
