<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Examination Latex and Nitrile Rubber Medical Gloves are essential tools used in the medical field for various procedures to maintain hygiene and prevent contamination. The gloves are made from latex or nitrile rubber materials, providing flexibility, dexterity, and protection for healthcare professionals from potential infections.</p>
