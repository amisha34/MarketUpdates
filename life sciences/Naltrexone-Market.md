<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Naltrexone implants are small, pellet-like formulations that are surgically inserted under the skin to provide a controlled release of the medication over an extended period. These implants are primarily used to manage alcohol and opioid dependencies by reducing cravings and blocking the euphoric effects of these substances.</p>
