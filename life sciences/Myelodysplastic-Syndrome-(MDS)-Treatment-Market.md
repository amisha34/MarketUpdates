<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Myelodysplastic Syndrome (MDS) is a group of disorders characterized by dysfunctional blood cell production in the bone marrow, leading to low blood cell counts. It can progress to acute myeloid leukemia if not treated promptly.</p>
