<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Oral Mesalazine, also known as 5-aminosalicylic acid, is a medication used to treat inflammatory bowel diseases such as ulcerative colitis and Crohn's disease. It works by reducing inflammation in the lining of the intestines.</p>
