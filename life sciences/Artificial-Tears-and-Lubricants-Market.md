<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Artificial Tears and Lubricants refer to eye drops or ointments used to relieve dryness and irritation in the eyes by mimicking the natural tears produced by the eye. These products are designed to supplement and maintain moisture on the eye's surface to alleviate discomfort and promote eye health. With the growing prevalence of conditions like dry eye syndrome and eye lubrication issues, the demand for artificial tears and lubricants continues to rise globally. </p>
