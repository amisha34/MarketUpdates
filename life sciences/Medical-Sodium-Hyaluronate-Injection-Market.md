<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Medical Sodium Hyaluronate Injection is a sterile, nonpyrogenic, viscoelastic solution designed for intra-articular injections to provide pain relief and improved joint function in patients suffering from osteoarthritis. This highly purified form of hyaluronic acid acts as a lubricant and shock absorber within the joint space.</p>
