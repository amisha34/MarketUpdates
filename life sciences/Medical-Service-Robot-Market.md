<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Medical service robots are autonomous machines designed to assist medical professionals in various tasks, such as surgery, patient care, and rehabilitation. These robots are equipped with advanced technologies like artificial intelligence and machine learning to enhance efficiency and precision in healthcare settings.</p>

<h2>Market Dynamics:</h2>
<ul>
<li>Rising Demand for Remote Healthcare Services: The increasing need for remote monitoring and care services has driven the adoption of medical service robots, especially in regions with limited access to healthcare facilities.</li>
<li>Technological Advancements in Robotics: Continuous advancements in robotic technologies, such as surgical robots and exoskeletons, have revolutionized healthcare delivery by enabling minimally invasive procedures and improved patient outcomes.</li>
<li>Growing Aging Population: The aging population globally has led to a greater demand for healthcare services, creating opportunities for medical service robots to assist in tasks like elder care and physical therapy.</li>
