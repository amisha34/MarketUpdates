<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>The Moving Die Rheometer (MDR) is a key instrument used in the rubber industry to measure the rheological properties of rubber compounds. It plays a crucial role in evaluating the processability, curing characteristics, and overall quality of rubber materials.</p>
