<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Neonatal Intensive Care Invasive Ventilators are devices designed to provide critical respiratory support to newborns and infants who are unable to breathe effectively on their own. These specialized ventilators are crucial in neonatal intensive care units to ensure optimal oxygenation and ventilation for neonates.</p>

<h2>Market Dynamics:</h2>
<ul>

  <li>Technological advancements in ventilator design, such as improved safety features and enhanced monitoring capabilities, are enhancing the efficiency of neonatal respiratory care.</li>
  <li>Rising investments in healthcare infrastructure, particularly in developing countries, are expected to bolster the market growth of neonatal ventilators.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Market Trends:</h2>
