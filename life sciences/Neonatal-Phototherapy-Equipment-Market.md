<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Neonatal Phototherapy Equipment refers to the devices used to treat newborn babies with jaundice by exposing them to specific wavelengths of light to break down excess bilirubin in the blood.</p>

<h2>Market Dynamics:</h2>
<ul>

  <li>Technological advancements in phototherapy equipment leading to more efficient and user-friendly devices.</li>
  <li>Increasing awareness about the importance of early jaundice management contributing to market growth.</li>
</ul>
