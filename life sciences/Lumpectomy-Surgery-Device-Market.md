<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Lumpectomy surgery devices are specialized tools used in minimally invasive procedures for the removal of cancerous or abnormal tissues from the breast, preserving the breast as much as possible. These devices play a crucial role in breast-conserving surgeries, offering patients a less invasive treatment option with faster recovery times and reduced scarring.</p>
