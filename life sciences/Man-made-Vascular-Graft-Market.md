<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Man-made Vascular Grafts are synthetic or biosynthetic materials used to replace, bypass, or enhance blood vessels in patients requiring vascular procedures. These grafts are crucial in treating various vascular diseases and improving blood circulation in the body.</p>
