<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Microwave diathermy units are medical devices that use electromagnetic waves in the microwave frequency spectrum to generate therapeutic heat within the body tissues. This modality is commonly utilized in physiotherapy for pain relief and muscle rehabilitation.</p>
