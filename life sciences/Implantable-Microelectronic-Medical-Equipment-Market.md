<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Implantable Microelectronic Medical Equipment refers to medical devices that are implanted inside the body to monitor and assist in the functioning of various bodily systems. These devices utilize microelectronic technology to provide continuous monitoring, delivery of therapies, and data collection for diagnostic purposes, enhancing healthcare outcomes and patient care.</p>
