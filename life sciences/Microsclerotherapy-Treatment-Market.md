<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Microsclerotherapy Treatment refers to a minimally invasive procedure used to treat small varicose veins and spider veins by injecting a sclerosing agent directly into the affected veins. This treatment aims to improve the appearance of these veins and alleviate associated symptoms. The procedure is performed under the guidance of ultrasound imaging to ensure precision and efficacy.</p>
