<p>Micro-RNAs are small non-coding RNA molecules that play significant roles in gene regulation and cellular processes. Micro-RNA Sequencing and Assay market involves the study and analysis of these small RNA molecules for diagnostic and therapeutic purposes, offering insights into disease mechanisms and potential treatment options.</p>
