<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Mortuary RefrigeratorMarket refers to the global market for specialized refrigeration units designed to preserve deceased bodies for longer durations, particularly in mortuaries, funeral homes, and other healthcare facilities. These refrigerators are essential for maintaining the appropriate temperature to prevent decomposition until the bodies are prepared for viewing or burial.</p>
