<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>The Medical Electric Vacuum Pump refers to a device that creates a vacuum using electricity, commonly used in medical settings for various applications such as suctioning fluids, wound drainage, and diagnostic procedures. This equipment plays a crucial role in maintaining aseptic conditions and facilitating medical procedures efficiently and effectively.</p>
